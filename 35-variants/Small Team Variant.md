# Small Team Variant

> [!summary] Fit
> Use this when multiple humans share ownership and the repo needs clearer review assignment and coordination.

## Additions

- CODEOWNERS or explicit ownership rules
- tighter PR expectations
- stronger branch protection
- merge queue if conflicts become frequent
- shared prompt and policy conventions

## Risks

- process fragments across people
- ownership becomes implicit
- agents inherit different rules from different humans
