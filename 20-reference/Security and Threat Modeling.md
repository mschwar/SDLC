# Security and Threat Modeling

> [!abstract] Definition
> The practice of identifying how the system can fail or be attacked, then shaping design and review around those risks.

## What To Cover

- auth and identity risks
- injection and data exposure risks
- secrets handling
- deployment and infrastructure risks
- agent permission misuse
