# Roadmaps and Backlogs

> [!abstract] Definition
> The prioritized record of intended work, sequencing, and strategic direction that the orchestrator uses to choose the next task.

## Good Roadmap Properties

- current enough to route work
- scoped enough to avoid vague goals
- linked to risks, constraints, or milestones
- small enough to stay readable
