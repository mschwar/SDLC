# Agentic-First

> [!abstract] Definition
> Design the workflow around autonomous execution, structural guardrails, and machine-readable validation instead of relying on people to remember process steps.

## What It Changes

- tasks become bounded objectives
- validation becomes executable
- handoffs become explicit
- self-review becomes structurally blocked
