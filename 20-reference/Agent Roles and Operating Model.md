# Agent Roles and Operating Model

> [!abstract] Definition
> The explicit description of which agents exist, what responsibilities they own, what they are allowed to do, and how they hand work to one another.

## Common Roles

- orchestrator
- clarifier or PM
- architect
- worker or coder
- tester
- reviewer
- deployer
- SRE
- governance

## Why It Matters

Without a role model, “multi-agent” systems collapse into overlapping responsibilities, weak accountability, and invisible approval paths.
