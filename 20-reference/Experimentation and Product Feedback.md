# Experimentation and Product Feedback

> [!abstract] Definition
> The process of validating whether a shipped change improved the product or business outcome it was meant to affect.

## Why It Matters

An end-to-end SDLC is incomplete if it stops at deploy. The loop closes only when the team learns whether the change helped.
