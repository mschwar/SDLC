# Separation of Duties

> [!abstract] Definition
> Distribute sensitive responsibilities so the same actor does not define, implement, approve, and deploy the same change unchecked.

## Why It Matters

Agentic workflows compress time. Separation of duties prevents that speed from collapsing all control into one execution path.

## Typical Splits

- worker vs reviewer
- deployer vs observer
- policy author vs policy executor
- spec approver vs implementer
