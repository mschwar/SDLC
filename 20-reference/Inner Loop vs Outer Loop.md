# Inner Loop vs Outer Loop

> [!abstract] Definition
> The inner loop is local work: code, test, fix. The outer loop begins after the change leaves the machine: CI, review, merge, deploy, observe.

## Why It Matters

Speed in the inner loop improves individual execution. Speed in the outer loop improves system throughput. Reliable SDLC design needs both.
