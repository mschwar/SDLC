# SLOs, SLIs, and Alerting

> [!abstract] Definition
> Service level indicators measure runtime behavior, service level objectives define acceptable targets, and alerting routes attention when those targets are threatened or breached.

## Why It Matters

Without reliability targets, observability can become passive data collection rather than a decision system.
