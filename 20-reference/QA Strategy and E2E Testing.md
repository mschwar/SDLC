# QA Strategy and E2E Testing

> [!abstract] Definition
> The intentional mix of unit, integration, end-to-end, UI, and exploratory validation used to prove that the system works at the right level.

## Good Strategy

- keep most checks low-cost and local
- add integration tests at important boundaries
- use E2E and UI checks where user workflows matter
- collect evidence that maps to actual risk
