# Risk Registers

> [!abstract] Definition
> A living list of known risks, their impact, mitigation plans, and triggers for escalation.

## Why It Matters

An agent-first workflow moves quickly. The risk register keeps hidden hazards visible enough to influence prioritization, review depth, and approval gates.
