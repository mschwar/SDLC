# Observability

> [!abstract] Definition
> The ability to understand system behavior through logs, metrics, traces, and business signals.

## What Agents Need

- machine-readable telemetry
- enough context to localize failures
- clear permission boundaries between observation and intervention

## Why It Matters

Without observability, deployment is a one-way action. With observability, deployment becomes a learning loop.
