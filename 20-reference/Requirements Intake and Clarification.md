# Requirements Intake and Clarification

> [!abstract] Definition
> The process of turning vague human intent into bounded, testable, implementation-ready work.

## Good Intake Output

- clear objective
- constraints
- non-goals
- acceptance criteria
- evidence expectations

## Why It Matters

Most agent failure begins upstream as ambiguous intent, not downstream as bad code.
