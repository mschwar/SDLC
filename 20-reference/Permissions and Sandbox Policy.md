# Permissions and Sandbox Policy

> [!abstract] Definition
> The rules that determine what agents can read, write, execute, and approve in each environment.

## Baseline Principles

- least privilege
- read-only production by default
- sandbox first
- explicit approval thresholds for irreversible actions
