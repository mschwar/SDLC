# Economics and Cost Tracking

> [!abstract] Definition
> The practice of measuring what the workflow costs in model usage, CI/runtime spend, human review effort, and time-to-merge.

## What To Track

- cost per merged change
- human touch-points per task
- review time
- compute or CI spend
- failed-run waste
