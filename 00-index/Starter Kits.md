## How To Use Them

1. Pick the smallest kit that matches your repo shape.
2. Copy the folder contents into a new repository.
3. Rename placeholder package and service names.
4. Replace the sample contracts and fixtures with real ones.
5. Keep the quality loop intact while you customize the rest.

## Relationship To Other Vault Layers

- Templates: reusable note or file fragments
- Starter kits: copyable repo skeletons
- Examples: fully worked reference implementations
